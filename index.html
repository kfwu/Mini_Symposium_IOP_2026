Registration

Important: All participants must complete online registration and payment before the deadline. On-site registration will be available only if capacity permits.

Abstract Submission

We invite all researchers to submit abstracts for contributed talks and poster presentations. The submission deadline is Date XXX.

Important: All abstracts must be submitted through the submission email. Please prepare your abstract in Word format.

Submission Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when preparing your abstract:

  • Maximum length: 1 page (A4)
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Title: Bold, centered
  • Author list: Include full names and affiliations

Topics

We welcome submissions on the following topics:

  • Strong Field Physics and Attosecond Science
  • Ultrafast X-ray Science
  • Quantum Control and Coherent Dynamics
  • Novel Light Sources and Instrumentation
  • Ultrafast Spectroscopy and Imaging
  • Theory and Computation in Ultrafast Science
  • And other topics intersted to the community
